Experience

Volunteering with COPCP (Cityline Ozone Park Civilian Patrol)

I volunteered with COPCP starting at the end of October of 2021. One of my mom's friends recommended it and I went to help out. After going a few times, I have never been able to stop myself from going unless I had something to do. In the harshest of weathers and no matter the situation, I donated my time and met some amazing people. The people I work with are like a secondary family and we have faced a lot. Through some of the coldest winters and now, with the hot and humid summer, we keep serving our community. Besides the food pantry, I volunteer at their own events and help as much as I can. I hope to keep working with them as long as the community needs us.
